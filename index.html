Rules & Regulations

Please read all the rules carefully before your scheduled event.

📹 Camera Setup

  • • The camera must remain stable throughout the entire solve.
  • • The following must be clearly visible at all times: cube, cube cover, timer, and both hands.
  • • The entire solving area should remain inside the camera frame.
  • • The cube must never go outside the camera frame during the attempt.

🧩 Before the Solve

  1. Apply the scramble exactly as provided.
  2. Scrambles will be sent one by one in the Google Meet in-call chat.
  3. Only solve the scramble that is currently provided.
  4. After scrambling, show any one side of the scrambled cube clearly to the camera.
  5. Wait for the organizer’s instruction to cover the cube.
  6. When instructed, place the cube under the cube cover.

▶️ Starting the Attempt

  • • The organizer will count “1...2...3...Start”.
  • • After “Start”, the official 15-second inspection begins.
  • • Complete your solve following all applicable WCA regulations.
  • • After finishing, show the timer clearly to the camera so your result is visible.

⚖️ WCA Regulations

  • • All official WCA Regulations are applicable throughout the competition.
  • • Penalties, +2, DNF, DNS, and all other decisions will be made according to the WCA Regulations.

❌ Attendance & Conduct

  • • Participants must join the Google Meet at their allotted time.
  • • If a participant does not join at the scheduled time, they will be disqualified (DQ) and all solves will be recorded as DNS.
  • • Do not receive any outside assistance during your attempts.
  • • Any form of cheating, unfair practice, or violation of the rules may result in immediate disqualification.
