Rules & Regulations

1. Inspection Time

Maximum 15 seconds of inspection per solve. Exceeding results in penalties as per WCA standards.

2. Penalties

+2 second penalties apply for slight errors; DNF (Did Not Finish) for incomplete/wrong solutions.

3. Camera Setup & Equipment

While you are on Google Meet, your cube, cube cover, and hands must be clearly visible on camera. You need a WCA-legal cube, a timer (such as Stackmat, CSTimer, or Twisty Timer), and a non-transparent cube cover or cloth.

4. WCA Rules Apply

All WCA rules and regulations are applicable for this event, and any violation will be handled accordingly.
